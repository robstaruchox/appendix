Qiacube Protocol for Fibrous Tissue Extraction

1. Add 450 l ethanol 96-100 % to 900 l sample lysate supernatant in S-Block

2. Mix 8 x and 1st transfer of 675 l lysed samples into RNeasy 96 Plate

3. ** Vacuum 25 kPa for 3 min

4. Mix 8 x and 2nd transfer of 675 l lysed samples into RNeasy 96 Plate

5. ** Vacuum 25 kPa for 3 min

6. *** Add 400 l RWT into RNeasy 96 Plate

7. *** Vacuum 25 kPa for 1 min

8. ***Add 80 l DNase-mix into RNeasy 96 Plate

9. *** 15 min incubation

10. *** Add 400 l RWT into RNeasy 96 Plate

11. *** Vacuum 25 kPa for 1 min

12. Add 600 l RPE into RNeasy 96 Plate

13. Vacuum 35 kPa for 1 min

14. Add 600 l RPE into RNeasy 96 Plate

15. Vacuum 35 kPa for 1 min

16. Add 400 l ethanol 96-100% into RNeasy 96 Plate

17. 2 min incubation

18. Vacuum 35 kPa for 1 min

19. 1 min incubation

20. Drying membrane with vacuum of 50 kPa for 10 min

21. Move transfer carriage to elution position

22 Add 55ul RNase-free Water into RNeasy 96 Plate

23. 5 min incubation

24. Vacuum 35 kPa for 1 min

25. Add 55ul RNase free Water into RNeasy 96

26. Add 30ul TopElute Fluid to into RNeasy 96 Plate

27. 1 min incubation

28. Vacuum 70 kPa for 5 min

29. Move transfer carriage to waste position

3) TopElute-Overlay Step TopElute Fluid can be used during elution of nucleic acids from the RNeasy membrane. It enables application of a stable and high vacuum and results in equal eluate volumes. In addition, TopElute Fluid eliminates the formation of drops of elution buffer at the outlet nozzles of the RNeasy 96 plate. By default, the Top Elute Overlay - option is checked. In case TopElute Fluid should not be used during the run, check the TopElute option under Advanced options. Important: Please ensure that you only take the eluate from below the top layer. Note: TopElute Fluid might be found as a top layer over the elution buffer. It is inert and has no effects on downstream applications.
